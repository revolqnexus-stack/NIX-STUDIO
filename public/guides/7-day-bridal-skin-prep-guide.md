## 📅 Before You Begin: The Prep Checklist

### ✅ Required Products:
- [ ] Oil-free cleanser
- [ ] Rose water toner
- [ ] Vitamin C serum (10% concentration)
- [ ] Hyaluronic acid serum
- [ ] Lightweight gel moisturizer
- [ ] SPF 50+ sunscreen (non-greasy)
- [ ] Night cream (gel-based)
- [ ] Under eye cream

### 🚫 What to Avoid:
- Heavy, oily moisturizers
- New products (within 2 weeks of wedding)
- Harsh scrubs or exfoliants
- Face steaming or saunas
- Oils or serums with heavy textures

## 🌤️ Day 1-2: Deep Cleansing Protocol

### **Morning (6:30 AM)**
1. **Cleanse:** Oil-free cleanser with lukewarm water
2. **Tone:** Rose water on cotton pad (avoid alcohol-based toners)
3. **Serum:** 3 drops Vitamin C serum (pat gently)
4. **Moisturize:** Pea-sized gel moisturizer
5. **Protect:** SPF 50+ sunscreen (wait 5 minutes before makeup)

### **Evening (8:00 PM)**
1. **Double Cleanse:** Oil-free cleanser twice
2. **Exfoliate:** Gentle scrub (Day 2 only)
3. **Tone:** Rose water toner
4. **Serum:** Vitamin C serum
5. **Moisturize:** Lightweight night cream

**💡 Kerala Tip:** Our humidity means your skin produces more oil. The double cleanse prevents buildup without stripping natural oils.

---

## 💧 Day 3-4: Hydration Balance

### **Morning (6:30 AM)**
1. **Rinse:** Cool water only (no cleanser)
2. **Tone:** Rose water toner
3. **Serum:** Vitamin C serum (2 drops only)
4. **Moisturize:** Gel moisturizer (very small amount)
5. **Protect:** SPF 50+ sunscreen

### **Evening (8:00 PM)**
1. **Cleanse:** Oil-free cleanser with lukewarm water
2. **Serum:** Hyaluronic acid (avoid over-application)
3. **Moisturize:** Gel night cream (pea-sized)
4. **Eye Cream:** Tap gently around eyes

**💡 Kerala Tip:** Most brides over-moisturize! In our humidity, lightweight hydration that absorbs completely is key.

---

## ✨ Day 5-6: Skin Perfecting

### **Morning (6:30 AM)**
1. **Rinse:** Cool water only
2. **Tone:** Rose water toner
3. **Serum:** Vitamin C serum
4. **Moisturize:** Gel moisturizer
5. **Protect:** SPF 50+ sunscreen

### **Evening (8:00 PM)**
1. **Cleanse:** Oil-free cleanser
2. **Mask:** DIY hydrating mask (see bonus section)
3. **Tone:** Rose water toner
4. **Serum:** Hyaluronic acid
5. **Moisturize:** Gel night cream
6. **Lip Care:** Lip balm before bed

**💡 Kerala Tip:** Day 5 is perfect for addressing any last-minute concerns. Don't try new treatments!

---

## 🌟 Day 7: Wedding Day Prep

### **Morning (5:00 AM - Before Makeup Artist Arrives)**
1. **Rinse:** Cool water only (NO cleanser)
2. **Tone:** Witch hazel toner (oil control)
3. **Serum:** NO serum (let skin rest)
4. **Moisturize:** NO moisturizer (unless very dry)
5. **Eye Cream:** Only if needed, apply 30 minutes before makeup
6. **Lips:** Lip balm (remove before makeup application)

### **Critical Wedding Day Rules:**
- NO new products
- NO heavy creams
- NO touching face
- NO stress (easier said than done!)

**💡 Kerala Tip:** Your skin should be clean but not "squeaky clean." A tiny bit of natural oil helps makeup adhere better.

## 🎁 Bonus: Emergency Treatments

### **Overnight Pimple Fix:**
1. Cleanse area with toner
2. Apply tea tree oil (diluted) with Q-tip
3. Cover with pimple patch
4. Ice for 2 minutes if swollen
5. Sleep with head elevated

### **DIY Kerala-Friendly Face Mask:**
**Ingredients:**
- 2 tbsp multani mitti (fuller's earth)
- 1 tbsp rose water
- 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/4 tbsp turmeric

**Instructions:**
Mix into smooth paste. Apply for 10 minutes. Rinse with cool water. Perfect for Day 5 evening!

### **Monsoon vs Summer Adjustments:**

**Monsoon (June-Oct):**
- Focus on oil control
- Use clay masks twice a week
- Avoid heavy moisturizers completely
- Blotting papers are your best friend

**Summer (Mar-May):**
- Extra hydration needed
- Use cooling gel masks
- SPF reapplication every 3 hours
- Keep face mists in fridge
