## Posting Strategy & Best Practices:

### Posting Schedule:
- **Frequency**: 2-3 posts per week
- **Timing**: 7-9 PM (when brides are online)
- **Duration**: Run for 4-6 weeks, then refresh

### Hashtag Strategy:
**Primary (high volume):**
#KottayamBride #BridalMakeup #WeddingPrep #KeralaWedding

**Secondary (niche):**
#BridalSkincare #WeddingGlow #MalayaliBride #BridalBeauty

**Location-specific:**
#KottayamSalon #KeralaBeauty #SouthIndianBride

### Image Requirements:
- **Size**: 1200x900 pixels (landscape)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional
- **Branding**: Include Nixstudio logo subtly

### Call-to-Action Optimization:
- Use clear, direct CTAs
- Create urgency when appropriate
- Make it easy to click through
- Track clicks with UTM parameters

### Engagement Strategy:
1. **Respond to all comments** within 24 hours
2. **Answer questions** thoroughly
3. **Share user-generated content** (with permission)
4. **Update offers** regularly to keep content fresh

### Performance Tracking:
- **Metrics to monitor**: Views, clicks, calls, direction requests
- **Goal**: 15-25% click-through rate to landing page
- **Adjust**: Test different headlines, images, CTAs based on performance

### Local SEO Benefits:
- Posts help with Google Maps ranking
- Fresh content signals relevance to Google
- Keywords in posts improve local search visibility
- Engagement metrics boost overall business ranking
