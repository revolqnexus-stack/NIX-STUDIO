## Ad Creative Recommendations:

### Image/Video Ideas:
1. **Before/After skin photos** (real clients, with permission)
2. **Behind-the-scenes** of Nikita doing makeup
3. **Text overlay** with key statistics ("200+ Brides Helped")
4. **Video testimonial** from happy bride
5. **Split screen**: Stressed bride vs. glowing bride

### Audience Targeting:
- **Location**: Kottayam + 50km radius
- **Age**: 22-32
- **Interests**: Wedding planning, bridal makeup, luxury beauty
- **Behavior**: Engaged shoppers, recent engagements
- **Custom audiences**: Website visitors, email list

### Budget Allocation:
- **Test budget**: ₹500/day for 3 days per variation
- **Scale winner**: ₹2,000-3,000/day
- **Target CPA**: Under ₹50 per lead
- **Expected conversion**: 15-25% to booking consultation

### A/B Testing Elements:
- Headline variations
- Image vs. video
- Different CTAs ("Download Now" vs. "Get Free Guide")
- Length of ad copy (short vs. detailed)

### Retargeting Strategy:
1. **Website visitors**: Show testimonial ads
2. **PDF downloaders**: Show consultation booking ads
3. **Engaged users**: Show behind-the-scenes content
