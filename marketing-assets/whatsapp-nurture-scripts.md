## Implementation Notes:

### WhatsApp Automation Setup Options:
1. **WhatsApp Business API** - For professional automation
2. **ManyChat/Chatfuel** - Third-party integrations
3. **Manual templating** - Copy-paste with personalization

### Best Practices:
- **Timing is crucial:** 24 hours after download shows you're attentive but not pushy
- **Personalization:** Use their actual name when possible
- **Value-first:** Always provide help before asking for anything
- **Local context:** Mention Kottayam/humidity to show you understand their specific challenges
- **Low-pressure:** Focus on helping, not selling

### Response Templates for Common Questions:

**If they ask about products:**
"Great question! For the routine, I recommend [specific product suggestions]. However, everyone's skin is different - would you like me to schedule a quick 10-minute call to discuss your specific skin type?"

**If they mention wedding date:**
"Wonderful! That gives us plenty of time to perfect your skin prep. I typically recommend starting the full bridal skincare routine 3 months before the wedding. When would be a good time to discuss your complete bridal makeup plan?"

**If they don't respond:**
No follow-up needed - you've planted the seed. They now have your WhatsApp number and will reach out when ready.
